Purpose:
  To perform a right arithmetical shift of a 16-bit integer in a register by a constant bit amount.

Restrictions:
  The amount may be between 0 and 15, inclusive.

Operation:
  opA ← mem[W|Ra]
  opB ← mem[W|Rb]
  res ← opA[15]{imm3+1}|opA[16:15-imm3]
  mem[W|Rd] ← res
  Z ← res = 0
  S ← res[15]
  C ← UNDEFINED
  V ← UNDEFINED
